Make Google your homepage in three steps:

  1. Click the gear icon
  2. Select Preferences from the menu.
  3. Set the following fields under the General tab:
    In the Home page field, type "www.google.com"
    For New windows opens with, select: Home Page
    For New tabs opens with, select: Home Page

Make Google your homepage in four steps:

  1. Navigate to the Tools menu at the top of the Opera browser and click on the Preferences menu.
  2. Under the General tab, go to Startup drop-down option, and choose Start with Home page.
  3. Type "www.google.com" in the Home page text box.
  4. Click OK at the bottom to make Google your homepage.

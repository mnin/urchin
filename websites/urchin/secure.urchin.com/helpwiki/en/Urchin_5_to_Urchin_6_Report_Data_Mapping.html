Urchin 5 to Urchin 6 Report Data Mapping

Urchin 5 Profile settings and reports with data aren't migrated to Urchin 6 fully as is:

  • Migration of Goals created in Urchin 5 is not supported.
  • "ELF" log format is not supported in Urchin 6 and logs of this format are skipped during migration.
  • Urchin 6 has different set of Visitor Tracking methods, compared to Urchin 5. The table below summarizes how these methods are converted after migration.

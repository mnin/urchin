Support Policies

Urchin Software from Google is not supported directly by Google. Rather, it is sold and supported exclusively by our partner network. Help is also available from the global Urchin community.

  • Urchin Help Forum (Google Group): This is a popular venue for posting challenging questions about Urchin deployments, usage, and performance tuning.
  • Urchin Software Authorized Consultants: If you purchased Urchin 6, you did so through one of our resellers, who provide all support for software they have sold.
  • Urchin 5 Support: Google provides limited email-based support for Urchin 5. Please note this service will be ending on July 1, 2008, after which point our partner network will provide limited email-based support. Contact urchin-support@google.com.

Looking for Google Analytics support? You can file a help ticket from the Google Analytics Help Center.
